---
title: "호텔 관련 영어"
---

**체크인 할 때**

- Choi 라는 이름으로 예약 했습니다.
  <EnglishSnippet>
    {"Hello, I have a reservation under the name of CHOI (Last Name), C-H-O-I."}
  </EnglishSnippet>

**체크인 전 짐 맡길 때**

- 저는 이 호텔에 머물 예정인데요. 체크인 전에 짐 좀 맡길 수 있을까요?

  <EnglishSnippet>
    {
      "I have a reservation at your hotel. I was wondering if I could leave my luggage before checking in?"
    }
  </EnglishSnippet>

  (Easy ver.)

  <EnglishSnippet>{"Can you store my bags, please?"}</EnglishSnippet>
  <EnglishSnippet>{"Can you keep my baggage?"}</EnglishSnippet>
  <EnglishSnippet>{"Can I leave my bags, please?"}</EnglishSnippet>

**맡긴 짐을 찾을 때**

- 제 가방을 찾을 수 있을까요?
  <EnglishSnippet>{"Can I get my bags back?"}</EnglishSnippet>

**공항 셔틀버스 관련**

- 공항까지 가는 셔틀버스 서비스가 있나요?

  <EnglishSnippet>{"I was wondering if there is a shuttle service to the airport?"}</EnglishSnippet>

- 요금은 어떻게 되나요?

  <EnglishSnippet>{"How much do you charge for the shuttle?"}</EnglishSnippet>
